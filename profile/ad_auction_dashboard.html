Ad Auction Intelligence Dashboard

A coursework project that transforms raw ad auction logs into interactive analytics. Upload a campaign bundle, explore KPIs by audience and context, and export insights with a single click—all governed by clear user roles.

What the Dashboard Delivers

Marketing analysts can upload zip files containing server_log, impression_log, and click_log. The system parses, enriches, and displays the combined dataset as slicing-friendly charts. Filters for age, user ID, context, and date range keep deep dives quick, while exports provide CSV data and PNG snapshots for slide decks.

  • Admins manage campaigns, invite teammates, and adjust global filters and default KPIs.
  • Editors tweak campaign settings and preset dashboards without touching access policies.
  • Viewers focus on analysis—zooming charts, comparing cohorts, and downloading assets for reports.
